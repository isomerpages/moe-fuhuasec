<p><strong>Fuhua SAC Good Progress Award</strong>
<br>The award is given to students who have made significant improvement in
the area of academic studies for the year. Students must also have demonstrated
good conduct. A total of 5 awards per level and course will be given out
annually. Each award is valued at $100 and will be presented at the school's
Awards Day.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>Fuhua SAC Support Scheme Award</strong>
<br>This award ensures that deserving students who are financially less advantaged
are provided with additional support to realise their potential and exemplify
the school’s vision. Students must be on the MOE / SAC financial assistance
scheme. The number of awards will be capped at 2 per level and course.
Each award is valued at $300 and will be presented at the school's Awards
Day.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Fuhua Spiritedness Award</strong>
<br>This special award is given to teachers and students who have exemplified
the school values in school and in the community.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Fuhua SAC Sportsmanship Award</strong>
<br>This award is given to a student who has exemplified outstanding character
and values in sports. The award is valued at $100 and will be presented
at the school's Awards Day.&nbsp;</p>
