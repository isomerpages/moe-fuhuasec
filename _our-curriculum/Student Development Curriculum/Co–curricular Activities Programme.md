<h3>Co-curricular Activities Programme</h3>
<p><strong>Vision</strong>
<br>Passionate and holistically developed Fuhuanites leading balanced lives</p>
<p><strong>Mission</strong>
<br>To develop students’ talents to their fullest potential while cultivating
upstanding character and developing their E21CC.</p>
<p>Our Co-curricular Activities (CCA) Programme provides Fuhuanites with
a platform to discover their interests, talents and to strive for excellence.
It aims to ignite in Fuhuanites a lifelong love for a particular activity,
allowing them to lead a balanced life in adulthood. Through active participation
in CCAs, Fuhuanites strengthen the cultivation of school values of Respect,
Integrity, Service and Excellence while developing friendships and collaborating
with other Fuhuanites.</p>
<p><strong>Approach</strong>
</p>
<p>Our CCA Programme is planned to achieve the following objectives:&nbsp;</p>
<p>1. Breadth of experiences that support students’ holistic development
and allow them to discover their interests and talents.
<br>2. Development and progression in the learning experiences that deepen
students’ knowledge and skills to fuel their lifelong love for the activity.
&nbsp;values and E21CC.</p>
<p>3. Develop leadership, teamwork and a spirit of service to the community
to help them face future challenges.</p>
<p>4. Develop character, values, social emotional competencies and E21CC.&nbsp;</p>
<p>5. Promotes social bonding and interaction among students from diverse
background, forming friendships and deepen their&nbsp; sense of belonging
to school and the community.</p>
<p>The specific knowledge, skills and competencies developed depends on the
choice of CCA. There are four broad clusters of CCAs: <a href="/co-curricular-activities/clubs-and-societies/overview/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Clubs and Societies</a>,

</p>
