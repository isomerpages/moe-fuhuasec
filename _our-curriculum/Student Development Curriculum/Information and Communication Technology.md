<h3>Information and Communication Technology</h3>
<p><strong>Vision</strong>
<br>Future-ready learner, digitally empowered innovator</p>
<p><strong>Mission</strong>
<br>To nurture the whole Fuhuanite through technology-empowered quality learning
experiences; To empower teachers in designing effective learning through
purposeful ICT integration</p>
<p><strong>Approach</strong>
<br>Teachers design ICT-aided learning experiences which promote self-directed
learning and collaborative learning based on the Student Learning Space
(SLS) Pedagogical Scaffold. Designed by MOE Educational Technology Division,
this model provides the parameters for teachers to consider when designing
a cognitively engaging learning experience which promotes studentsâ€™ thinking,
discourse and articulation of ideas and sharing of feedback.</p>
<p><strong>Key Programmes</strong>
<br>
<br><strong>(1) Digital Skills Programme for all Secondary 1 students</strong>
<br>All Secondary 1 students will attain a baseline standard of ICT proficiency
as well as Communication, Collaboration and Information skills after completing
the in-house curriculum.</p>
<p><strong>(2) Coding Programme for Secondary 1 and 2 students</strong>
<br>All Secondary 1 and 2 students are enrolled in a coding programme of 8
to 10 hours, to achieve the following:&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Nurturing students to be curious about technology, confident in using
technology and creative in solving problems using technology</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Supporting the development of digital competencies and technological skills
such as Computational Thinking, Coding and Programming, Digital Knowledge
Currency and Artificial Intelligence Literacy.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Supporting the development of Emerging 21st Century Competencies such
as Critical, Adaptive and Inventive Thinking.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>(3) Enhanced Cyber and Digital Wellness Education Package</strong>
</p>
<p>Cyber and Digital Wellness education is provided for all students. It
comprises the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Cyber and Digital Wellness lesson packages, assembly programmes and class
projects that focus on contemporary issues</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cyber and Digital Wellness Leadership and Advocacy Programme for Student
Ambassadors</p>
</li>
</ul>
