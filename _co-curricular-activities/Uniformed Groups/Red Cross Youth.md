<h3>Red Cross Youth</h3>
<h5>About Us</h5>
<p><strong>Vision</strong>&nbsp;
<br>Confident and competent youths who are leaders within their communities,
serving humanity and furthering the work of the Red Cross Movement.</p>
<p><strong>Mission</strong>
<br>&nbsp;Committed to engaging our youths in various service learning and
humanitarian programmes, and giving them opportunities at National and
International platforms</p>
