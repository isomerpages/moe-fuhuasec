<h3>Robotics Club</h3>
<h5>About Us</h5>
<p><strong>Vision</strong>&nbsp;
<br>Inspiring young minds to be resilient and collaborative</p>
<p><strong>Mission</strong>
<br>&nbsp;To promote creativity, perseverance and critical thinking in our
students through self-directed, hands-on problem-based learning.</p>
<h5>OUR Key Programmes</h5>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Competition Trainings: National Robotics Competition, World Robot Olympiad;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Student-Led Robotics Workshop and VIA Project;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Annual Camp and School Open House</p>
</li>
</ul>
