<h3>Infocomm Club</h3>
<h5>About Us</h5>
<p><strong>Vision</strong>&nbsp;
<br>Excite. Express. Experience. Nurture students to become technology-driven
individuals.</p>
<p><strong>Mission</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To excite students' learning with Info-communication Technologies;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To express students' creativity through Photography and Videography;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To experience behind-the-scene production in events management</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h5>OUR Key Programmes</h5>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Photography modules</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Videography modules</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Elective programmes in web-designing and/or AVA</p>
</li>
</ul>
