<h3>Table Tennis</h3>
<h5>About Us</h5>
<p><strong>Vision</strong>&nbsp;
<br>Where Character and Excellence Meet</p>
<p><strong>Mission</strong>
<br>&nbsp;To develop good sportsmanship and passion towards in table tennis.</p>
<h5>OUR Key Programmes</h5>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Annual sports selection for the Secondary One students&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Secondary One Welcome Tea</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Annual West Zone Inter-school Table Tennis Championships and National
Rounds&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Friendly matches with schools to sharpen skills and techniques&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop leadership qualities through planned activities by external vendor
and school&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Showcase our CCA achievements and spirit at our booth during annual CCA
Open House&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Annual table tennis camp to foster team work and character development</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Weekly training practices with the guidance of our coaches</p>
</li>
</ul>
