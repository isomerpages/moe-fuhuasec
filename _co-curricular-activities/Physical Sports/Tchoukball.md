<h3>Tchoukball</h3>
<h5>About Us</h5>
<p><strong>Vision</strong>
<br>Developing an all-round student-athlete through Tchoukball CCA</p>
<p><strong>Mission</strong>
<br>To nurture our young student-athletes holistically by providing them with
exposure to competitive team sports, developing their competence in Tchoukball,
as well as, develop their sportsmanship and passion for Tchoukball and
other sports.</p>
<h5>OUR Key Programmes</h5>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Participation in annual National Inter-School Tchoukball Championships,
Trident Cup and Tchoukball School League</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Friendly matches with secondary schools and tertiary institutions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Annual mid-year and end-of-year Tchoukball Camp focusing on team-bonding
and character building</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Termly bonding sessions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sports leadership opportunities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sports VIA with external organisations</p>
</li>
</ul>
